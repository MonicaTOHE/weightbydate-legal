Privacy Policy — Weight By Date

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Who we are

Weight By Date ("the app", "we", "us") is a calorie calculator and weight-tracking application developed as a sole-developer project. Contact: your-email@example.com

2. What data we collect — and where it stays

Weight By Date does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter (weight, age, height, goal date, calorie plan) is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (AsyncStorage) and never leaves it.

We do not have a backend. We do not have a database. We cannot see your data.

3. Third-party services

The only third party integrated into this app is RevenueCat, used solely to process in-app subscription purchases through Google Play. When you make a purchase, Google Play and RevenueCat handle the transaction. RevenueCat receives a randomly generated user ID (not linked to your name or email) to validate your subscription status.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Health data

The app processes health-related inputs (weight, height, age, sex, activity level) locally on your device to calculate calorie recommendations. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Children (COPPA)

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. The app enforces a minimum age of 13 at profile creation.

6. Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive Android permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location, external storage).

7. Data deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by using the "Clear all" button inside the app, or by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions: your-email@example.com
